WINDOWS 10

1.Connect a blank USB flash drive or insert a blank writable DVD.

2.Make sure you have a product key.

3.Go to https://www.microsoft.com/en- us/software-download/windows10%20.

4.Click Download tool now

5.Double-click the downloaded file.

6.Click Accept to accept the license.

7.Select "Create installation media" and click OK

8.Select your preferences and click Next

9.Choose an installation type and click Next

10.Create your installation media.
