Computer Network Concept

The term network is any interconnected group of people or things capable of sharing meaningful information with one another. But in a technology context, network is usually short for "computer network" or "data network" and implies that computers are the things sharing the meaningful information. And basically, computer networks are the basis of communication in IT. Computer networks arc used in a huge variety of ways and can include many different types of network. It is 9 set of computers that are connected together So that they can share data and information. At a conceptual level, all data networks are consisting of nodes, which refers to any computer or digital device using the network and links and the physical connections (either wired or wireless) that carry messages between nodes.

Computer networks are used to carry out a large number of tasks through the sharing of data and information. And some of the things that networks are used for includes;

Communicating using email, video, instant messaging and other methods;

Sharing devices such as printers, scanners and photocopiers;

Sharing files;

Sharing software and operating programs on remote systems; and

Allowing network users to easily access and maintain information.

A computer networks can be categorized according to their sizes. A computer network mainly of four types:

LAN (Local Arca Network)

PAN (Personal Arca Network)

MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

WAN (Wide Area Network)

LAN (Local Area Netwerk)

Local Area Network group of computers that is connected to each other in a small area such as building or office.

It is used for connecting two or more personal computers through a communication medium like twisted pair, coaxial cable. etc.

It is cheaper compared to any other network as it is built with inexpensive hardware such as hubs. network adapters, and ethernet cables.

The data is transferred at an extremely faster rate in LAN.

Local Area Network provides higher security because the data rotates in small group only.

MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

A metropolitan area network covers larger geographic area. It is composed of a different LAN to form a larger network.

Government agencies use this kind of network to connect to the citizens and private industries.

In MAN, various LANs are connected to each other through a telephone exchange line.

WAN (Wide Area Network)

WAN is a network that extends over a large geographical area such as states or countries.

A Wide Area Network is quite bigger network than the LAN, MAN and PAN.

WAN is not limited to a single location, but it spans over a large geographical area through a telephone line, fibre optic cable or satellite links.

The internet is one of the biggest WAN in the world.

A Wide Area Network is widely used in the field of Business, government, and education.

PAN (Personal Area Network)

PAN is a network arranged within an individual person, typically within a range of 10 meters.

PAN is used for connecting the computer devices for personal use.

Thomas Zimmerman was the first research scientist to bring the idea of the Personal Area Network.

Personal computer devices that are used to develop the personal area network are the laptop, mobile phones, media player and play stations.

There are two types of Personal Area Network:

Wired Personal Area Network - is created by using the USB.
